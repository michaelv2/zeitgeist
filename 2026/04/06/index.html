Geopolitics

Iran / Middle East Conflict

  • Active US-Iran-Israel military conflict is the #1 near-term risk to portfolios. Key variables:
    • Ceasefire scenario (45-day proposal): Oil spike partly reverses; gold softens; equities rally. Short-duration, watch for re-escalation.
    • Hormuz closure scenario: WTI could spike toward $120–$130+; airlines ($JETS), chemicals, and consumer discretionary get crushed; energy ($XLE, $XOP) surges; defense ($XAR) outperforms; bonds rally on growth fears.
    • Power plant strikes (Trump threatened): Could trigger Iranian retaliation on regional infrastructure, pulling Saudi/UAE into risk.
  • Markets are currently pricing uncertainty — oil elevated but not in "closure priced" territory; gold actually down, implying partial ceasefire hope.
