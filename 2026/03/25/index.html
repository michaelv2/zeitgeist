  • Iran's "non-hostile ship" statement is partial de-escalation — not a ceasefire. Drone warfare ongoing; US carrier groups deployed.
  • Net positioning: Energy ($XLE, $DBC, oil futures) remains a hedge until ceasefire confirmed. If deal materializes, oil drops sharply — be ready to reduce energy exposure quickly. Defense ($XAR) benefits regardless.
  • Israel involvement (striking Lebanon bridges) adds another vector of escalation risk.
