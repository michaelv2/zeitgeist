Key Themes

  • The Fed is changing hands into a hawkish setup, and the equity tape isn't pricing the policy-mistake risk. Two implications: fade the energy/defense-on-war-headline reflex on crude, and treat lower oil as a disinflationary offset that should cool the next CPI prints even as today's headline looks alarming.
