  • If Strait is fully disrupted or closed even temporarily: WTI could spike to $130+, triggering a stagflation shock. Energy ($XLE, $DBC) is a direct hedge; airlines ($JETS) and consumer discretionary are most exposed on the downside.
  • Prediction markets implying ~51% Trump leaves office before ~mid-2027 — elevated political uncertainty, but likely moonshot bias; discount meaningfully.

Russia-Ukraine

  • 3-day ceasefire is symbolic, tied to Victory Day. No structural peace framework. Putin's scaled-back parade signals resource strain; market should not price permanent ceasefire yet.
  • Putin removal by EOY 2026: 11.5% probability — low but non-trivial; a leadership change in Russia would be a massive geopolitical shock. European defense ($XAR equivalent) remains bid.

China-Taiwan

  • Prediction market at ~50% China invades Taiwan before ~mid-2027 — this is almost certainly severe moonshot bias. Xi removal before 2027 priced at only 7%, consistent with stable CCP leadership. Near-term Taiwan invasion probability is materially lower than 50%.

UK / Europe
